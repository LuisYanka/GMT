Seminar of Geometric Measure Theory

Welcome to the webpage of GMT!

Fall 2024

In this seminar, we mainly discuss the book "Introduction to Geometric Measure Theory" by L. Simon (we take [Sim14] as the abbreviation in the following table). We also have refer the book "Measure Theory and Fine Properties of Functions" for complements. The reading seminar is held mostly every Thursday and Sunday afternoon from 2 to 4 p.m. at the Jiayibing or Zhihua buildings of Peking University. Sometimes, due to the special schedules of the attendants, the time may change, and the seminar will be held online.

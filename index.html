Fall 2024

In this seminar, we mainly discuss the book "Introduction to Geometric Measure Theory" by L. Simon (abbreviated as [Sim14] in the following table). We also refer to the book "Measure Theory and Fine Properties of Functions" for additional references. The reading seminar is held mostly every Thursday and Sunday afternoon from 14:00 to 16:00 at the Jiayibing or Zhihua buildings of Peking University. Occasionally, due to the special schedules of the attendees, the time may change, and the seminar may be held online.

Attendees (Alphabetical)

Haotong Fu (Ph.D. candidate, PKU), Huaijie Wang (Postdoctoral fellow, PKU), and Wei Wang (Ph.D. candidate, PKU).

Spring 2023

In this seminar, we primarily study recent breakthroughs in harmonic maps surrounding the following two papers:
  • "Quantitative Stratification and the Regularity of Harmonic Maps and Minimal Currents" by Jeff Cheeger and Aaron Naber (abbreviated as [CN13])
  • "Rectifiable-Reifenberg and the Regularity of Stationary and Minimizing Harmonic Maps" by Aaron Naber and Daniele Valtorta (abbreviated as [NV17])
The reading seminar is held mostly every Friday evening from 18:40 to 20:40 in Science Building No. 2 of Peking University.

Attendees (Alphabetical)

Yuxuan Chen (Ph.D. student, PKU), Haotong Fu (Undergraduate student, PKU), Yuning Liu (Professor, NYU Shanghai), Wei Wang (Ph.D. student, PKU), and Zhifei Zhang (Professor, PKU).

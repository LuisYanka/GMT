Seminar of Geometric Measure Theory

Welcome to the webpage of GMT!

Fall 2024

In this seminar, we mainly discuss the book "Introduction to Geometric Measure Theory" by L. Simon (we take [Sim14] as the abbreviation in the following table). The reading seminar is held mostly every Sunday afternoon from 2 to 4 p.m. at the Jiayibing or Zhihua buildings of Peking University. Sometimes, due to the special schedules of the attendants, the time may change, and the seminar will be held online.
Details of contents Time (Day) Lecturer

Ch. 3 of [Sim14]: Preliminary results of BV functions 2024/12/27 Wei Wang
Ch. of [Sim14]: Reduced boundaries of sets with finite perimeters 2024/12/28 Wei Wang
